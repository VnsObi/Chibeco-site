About us

Chibeco Oil and Gas Nigeria Limited is a wholly Nigerian owned Oil and Gas trading company with headquarters in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. The Company was incorporated as a limited liability company under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria on 4th September, 2001 and was previously called Chibeco Merchants Nigeria Limited. The Company began operations the same month with undiversified scope of services including; procurement, equipment supplies, sale and haulage of marine products. The Company subsequently changed its name by way of a special resolution on the 4th of April, 2013. The company has grown from its initial staff strength of five people in 2001 to over fifty employees and now sits on a 24,411.5 square meter facility located at KM16 Port Harcourt/ Aba Express way, Port Harcourt, Rivers State. The Company is recognised as a major player in the downstream sector of Nigerian oil and gas industry and continues to strive in its commitment to be a leading brand in the international oil and gas market.

Our Mission

Our commitment is to offer best quality petroleum products at competitive prices in the exact specifications requested by our clients in record time.

Our Vision

We strive to be recognized as the leading indigenous bunker trading company locally and internationally.
